Chicken Parmesan

Description of the Recipe

Chicken Parmesan features chicken breasts that are coated in breadcrumbs, and may be quickly pan fried to create a little crunch. The breasts are then placed in a baking dish and topped with mozzarella cheese and tomato sauce.

Ingredients

  • 4 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves
  • Salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste
  • 2 large eggs
  • 3/4 cup grated parmesan cheese, divided
  • 2 tablespoons all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup olive olive
  • 1/2 cup prepared tomato sauce
  • 1/4 cup fresh mozzarella
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh basil
  • 1/2 cup grated provolone cheese
  • 2 teaspoons olive oil

Steps

  1. Fill a pan with about an inch of olive oil and let it heat up.
  2. Pound chicken to an even thickness.
  3. Season the chicken with salt and pepper.
  4. Add parmesan to the breadcrumbs.
  5. Bread the chicken and let it rest for about 15 mintues before cooking.
  6. Fry the chicken for about two to three minutes on both sides.
  7. Put sauce on top of the chicken, as well as the cheese.
  8. With a 450 F oven, place chicken in until the cheese begins to melt.
  9. Top with basil and serve hot.
